Return & Exchange Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not happy with a product, we're here to help.

Return Window

You can return or exchange items within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for Return

  • Product must be unused and in original condition.
  • All original tags, packaging, and invoice must be included.
  • Returns not applicable on innerwear, custom items, or gift cards.

How to Return?

  1. Go to “My Orders” and click on “Return Item”.
  2. Select reason and upload image if required.
  3. Our courier partner will pick it up in 2–4 days.

Refund Process

Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.
